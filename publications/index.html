Journals

2025

  1. GeoNordeste
    AVALIAÇÃO DA ACURÁCIA NA CLASSIFICAÇÃO DE USO E COBERTURA DA TERRA: UM ESTUDO DE CASO NA REGIÃO INTERMEDIÁRIA DE SÃO LUÍS COM O MAPBIOMAS
    Thomas Victor de Sousa Malheiros Rocha, Sérgio Souza Costa, Luís Fernando Fernando Cirqueira da Silva Correia, and 1 more author
    Revista GeoNordeste, Dec 2025
    Abs DOI Bib HTML

    A classificação de uso e cobertura da terra é essencial para gerenciar recursos naturais. Este estudo analisou a acurácia de classes como Mangue, Apicum, Praia e Duna dentro do agrupamento SA-23-Z, que cobre 72% da região intermediária de São Luís. Os resultados mostraram alta acurácia para Praia e Duna, apesar de limitações em outras classes, indicando que a classificação do MapBiomas apresenta uma ótima acurácia geral para a região.

  2. AABC
    The Amazon COP and the Need for Integration between the Environmental and Climate Agendas
    Denilson da Silva Bezerra, Sérgio Souza Costa, and others
    Anais da Academia Brasileira de Ciências, Sep 2025
    Abs DOI Bib HTML

    This letter discusses the 30th United Nations Climate Change Conference (COP30) in Belém, Brazil. It highlights the unique opportunity to unify environmental and climate agendas, focusing on five central points: protection of ecosystems, nature-based solutions (NbS), climate justice for indigenous populations, the importance of blue carbon, and practical goals for reducing emissions.

2020

  1. RSP
    Population-based seroprevalence of SARS-CoV-2 and the herd immunity threshold in Maranhão
    Antônio Augusto Moura da Silva, Lídio Gonçalves Lima-Neto, Conceição de Maria Pedrozo e Silva de Azevedo, and 18 more authors
    Revista de Saúde Pública, Dec 2020
    Abs DOI Bib HTML

    This population-based household survey estimated the seroprevalence of SARS-CoV-2 in the state of Maranhão, Brazil. The study found a seroprevalence of 40.4%, one of the highest reported at the time, suggesting that the local herd immunity threshold might have been approached during that period. The results also highlighted the effectiveness of non-pharmaceutical interventions such as mask-wearing and social distancing.

  2. RBIE
    Um Relato de Experiência da Adaptação de um Evento Acadêmico Presencial para o Contexto Virtual em Tempos de Pandemia
    Luis Rivero, Carlos Salles, Tiago Bonini, and 2 more authors
    Revista Brasileira de Informática na Educação, Dec 2020
    Abs DOI Bib HTML

    Este artigo apresenta os resultados da adaptação da Jornada de Informática do Maranhão (JIM) para o formato virtual devido à pandemia da COVID-19. O trabalho discute o planejamento, execução e avaliação do evento, que contou com maratonas de programação, webinários e competições de e-sports. Os resultados indicaram uma avaliação positiva dos participantes, destacando as vantagens do formato gratuito e a disponibilidade do conteúdo para consultas futuras.

  3. Ci.Inf.
    DBacademic: Conectando os dados abertos das instituições de ensino do Brasil
    Sérgio Souza Costa, Mateus Vitor Duarte, Micael Lopes Silva, and 2 more authors
    Ciência da Informação, Sep 2020
    Abs DOI Bib HTML

2019

  1. Geonorte
    Publicação de Dados Conectados para Modelos de Uso e Cobertura da Terra (Linked Data Publication for Land Change Models)
    Deyvison Aguiar Garcia, Sérgio Souza Costa, and Evaldinolia Gilbertoni Moreira
    Revista Geonorte, Dec 2019
    Abs DOI Bib HTML

    Land-use and land-cover change models utilize a large volume of data. This paper presents the integration of different tools and concepts into a methodology that allows for the reuse and recreation of databases across different computing environments. The methodology was applied to the construction and publication of a database for the Brazilian Amazon, using the QGIS geographic information system to demonstrate the approach. All data and codes used are published to allow for the replication of the experiments described.

2017

  1. RBC
    DBCells: An Open and Global Multi-Scale Linked Cells
    Sergio Souza Costa, Micael Lopes Silva, Thamyla Maria de Souza Lima, and 1 more author
    Revista Brasileira de Cartografia, May 2017
    Abs DOI Bib HTML

    Land change models require large amounts of data and are difficult to reproduce or reuse. Initiatives to open and link data increase the reproducibility of scientific experiments and data reuse. A core pillar of the linked data concept is the use of Uniform Resource Identifiers (URIs). In this paper, we propose DBCells: an architecture for the publication of a global cellular space where each cell has a URI. This approach allows for the comparison, reproduction, and reuse of models and data.

  1. Landsc Ecol
    Dynamical coupling of multiscale land change models
    Evaldinolia Moreira, Sérgio Souza Costa, Ana Paula Aguiar, and 2 more authors
    Landscape Ecology, Nov 2009
    Abs DOI Bib HTML

    This paper proposes a software organization for building computational models that support dynamical linking of multiple scales, coupling different types of models such as cell-space and agent-based models. We demonstrate how results can flow in both bottom-up and top-down directions, allowing feedback from local actors to regional scales in the Brazilian Amazonia.
